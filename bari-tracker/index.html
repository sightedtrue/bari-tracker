Welcome, Lele 👋

Bari Tracker is a private daily log for your post-bypass routine. Everything stays on this device.

  • Today — log foods & water, see your protein, calories, water progress.
  • Meds — daily checklist (omeprazole, multivitamin, calcium, omega-3) and your schedule.
  • Journal — quick symptoms log, smoking quit streak.
  • History — calendar of past days, weekly summary, 30-day trends.
  • Settings — adjust wake-up time, targets, meds, export/import data.

Add to your iPhone home screen via Share → “Add to Home Screen” for an app-like experience.
